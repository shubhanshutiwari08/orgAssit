Our Mission

OrgAssist is on a mission to revolutionize organizational efficiency by providing an intelligent virtual assistant that empowers employees, streamlines communication, and unlocks the full potential of our organization.

Our Vision

OrgAssist envisions a future where every employee thrives in a seamless and empowering work environment.

AI-powered Q&A

Answers questions about company policies, procedures, and practices. Chatbot Interface: Provides a user-friendly way to interact and get information. Advanced Search: Makes finding critical documents and information effortless. Boosts Productivity: Saves time by streamlining communication and information retrieval. Elevates Experience: Empowers employees and fosters a culture of innovation.

Techniques

Large Language Model: Understands and answers questions in natural language. NLP: Cracks your questions' codes to figure out what you really need. Information Retrieval: Hunts down the info you need from your organization's treasure trove. Machine Learning: Gets smarter over time, learning from your interactions. Dialogue Management: Keeps your chatbot conversations flowing smoothly.

Technologies

Large Language Models (LLMs) Natural Language Processing (NLP) Information Retrieval Machine Learning (ML)

Process

User asks a question via text or interacts with the chatbot interface. NLP kicks in: OrgAssist analyzes the user's question, understanding its intent and any keywords related to company policies, procedures, or practices. Information retrieval: OrgAssist searches its knowledge base (documents, databases) using the extracted keywords. Delivery: The answer is delivered to the user through text or the chatbot interface, depending on how the user interacted.
